Information About Us

Our Yoga Shaala Studio

In Sanskrit, the word Yoga Shaala means a place to learn Yoga. Yoga Shaala is located at the heart of Muscat in the Sultanate of Oman. We provide you a clean, convenient and comfortable environment to practice yoga and explore more. We are proud to offer different types of yoga class for practitioners of all levels from beginner to advanced by internationally recognized and qualified yoga teachers. We are a team of an inspirational and dedicated tribe of teachers, our expertise, experience, and passion shine through in the classroom.
At Yoga Shaala, we are dedicated to give people the most comprehensive and authentic yoga experience with our team of experienced Yoga Teachers from India. Yoga Shaala is a sanctuary where members can immerse in a complete wellness experience. We believe in the power of yoga to help people lead happier, healthier and more balanced lives. With a community of dedicated and passionate teachers Yoga Shaala offers exceptional teaching, facilities and environment that inspires people of all ages and abilities to make yoga part of their lives.
